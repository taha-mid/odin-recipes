Borhani Recipe

The digestive drink

Borhani is a drink mostly found in southeast asia region drank by people who can't a clear stomach before starting a feast

Ingredients

  • 1 kg yogurt
  • Four tablespoons of sugar
  • One tablespoon of coriander leaves
  • One tablespoon of mint leaves
  • One teaspoon of chilli
  • One tablespoon of tomato sauce
  • 1/2 tablespoons fried cumin powder
  • One tablespoon of fried coriander
  • 1tsp white pepper powder
  • One tablespoon of salt

Steps

  1. You've got to get a good grip on a bowl.
  2. Now you need to add all the above ingredients in a bowl of cold water.
  3. Now we have to take the soaked spices.
  4. Now, if you mix the yogurt and the spices, it will be ready to be made. Check the sugar salt and concentration and serve cold.
